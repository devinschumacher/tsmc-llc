About TSMC, LLC ("SERP")

TSMC, LLC is a diversified digital brand specializing in creating and growing online business ventures. Founded in August 2023, TSMC serves as the parent company behind several successful digital initiatives - including the SERP family of brands, and formerly SCHUMACHER INC & SERP Agency.

SERP Agency

Our story began in April 2019 with the creation of SERPCO, LLC - also known as SERP Agency â€” a digital marketing agency dedicated to helping businesses thrive online. Under the leadership of co-founder Devin Schumacher, SERP Agency quickly flourished, achieving annual revenues between $2-$3.5 million. SERP Agency was bootstrap funded from zero to over 25 employees, successfully assisting more than 1300 businesses across 217 industries, collectively generating over a quarter-billion dollars in new revenue.

In 2024, SERP Agency was successfully sold in a multi 7-figure acquisition, marking a significant milestone. As part of this strategic transaction, ownership interests were reorganized for efficiency and optimal structuring, with TSMC, LLC becoming the main entity to represent various brands.

Today, TSMC,LLC (operating colloquially under the trade name "SERP") continues to build upon the "SERP" brand legacy as a digital growth consultancy - offering growth & automation products and services to businesses internationally - including a sister company SERP AI - which provides artificial intelligence research and software development to create software and AI that helps power the products and services of SERP.

Devin Schumacher currently operates as the CEO of TSMC,LLC and is working towards building a brand that strives to provide free education, tools and resources to anyone who is interested in artificial intelligence, programming, digital marketing, and entrepreneurship.
